ABOUT us

Fulfill Rich (F-R) allows you to discover more than your usual supermarket ingredient. Recent assessments of the potential of large-scale entomophagy (eating insects) have led some experts to suggest insects as a potential alternative protein source to conventional livestock (beef, lamb, goat etc), citing possible benefits including greater efficiency, lower resource use, increased food security, and environmental and economic sustainability.

Its founder (Mrs Efunsile) originates from D.R.Congo. A land that holds more than 8 million habitant, Liaka Bien! (the company slogan, in English: eat well!) is crucial to survive. She was inspired by the fact that eating in her homeland is a journey of discovering, especially with insects. More than 50 recipes have been passed on from generations to generations, promoting a nutritious eating at a low cost.

The combination of knowledge and traditional cooking skills, will ensure that your visit to our site will in crave and enrich your cooking skills. From cooking on charcoal to steaming using banana leaves, or simply using frying pan, F-R wants you to be a part of it all, come and be part of our borderless land of taste
